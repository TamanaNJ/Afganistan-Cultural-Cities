Afghan cities each hold unique cultural treasures from traditional foods and clothes to historic landmarks and poetry. Explore and celebrate the diversity of Afghan culture.

Herat: Known for poetry, art, and the famous Friday Mosque.
Ghazni: Capital of Islamic culture and historic architecture.
Ghor: Home to the ancient Minaret of Jam.
Kabul: Capital city rich in history and markets.
Mazar: Renowned for the Blue Mosque and Nowruz.
Bamyan: Known for ancient Buddha statues and valleys.
